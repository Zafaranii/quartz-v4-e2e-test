3. **Workflow installs dependencies** (npm ci)
4. **Workflow runs type checking** (tsc)
5. **Workflow builds Quartz** (npx quartz build)
6. **Workflow deploys to GitHub Pages**
7. **Site is live** at `https://zafaranii.github.io/IBM-App-Connect-Common-Knowledge-Session/`
